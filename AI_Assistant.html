Project: AI & Automations

Overview

Within the corporate world, many tasks are repetitive and time-consuming. In my role, I focused on automating document processes to improve efficiency.

Using Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, and Selenium, I automated report generation, formatting, and error analysis. Additionally, I integrated existing APIs and frameworks to incorporate AI, creating a virtual assistant powered by GPT-4 that could execute these automation scripts seamlessly.
